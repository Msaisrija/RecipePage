Biryani Recipe

Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish made with aromatic spices, rice, and marinated meat or vegetables. This recipe brings you a delicious and traditional taste of biryani, perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup oil or ghee
  • Spices: 2 cinnamon sticks, 4 cloves, 4 cardamom pods, 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves for garnish
  • 3 cups water

Instructions

  1. Wash the basmati rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain before cooking.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a large pot. Add cumin seeds, cinnamon sticks, cloves, cardamom pods, and bay leaf. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown. Then add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and salt, then mix well.
  5. Add chicken pieces and yogurt. Cook until the chicken is tender and coated with spices.
  6. Add drained rice to the pot. Pour 3 cups of water, add garam masala, and bring it to a boil.
  7. Once it starts boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and cook for 20-25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked.
  8. Turn off the heat and let the biryani rest for 5 minutes. Fluff the rice with a fork.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander and mint leaves before serving.
